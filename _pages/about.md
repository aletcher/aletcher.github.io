**Interests:** AI safety, reinforcement & multi-agent learning, world models, game theory, evolution strategies. Currently working on (1) extracting world models from model-free agents, and (2) implicitly promoting cooperation among self-interested AI agents using indirect reciprocity (aka reputation). Nerd for math puzzles like [EM sequence density](https://mathenchant.wordpress.com/2019/10/16/guess-again-the-ehrenfeucht-mycielski-sequence/).

**Previously:** Quantum research @ [IBM Research Zurich](https://www.zurich.ibm.com) and professional contemporary dancer @ [Malpelo Dance Company](https://www.malpelo.org/). MSc in [Mathematics & Theoretical CS](https://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/courses/msc-mathematics-and-foundations-computer-science) @ University of Oxford.
